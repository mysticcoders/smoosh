Smoosh Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy

We don't collect anything.

Smoosh is a local-only macOS utility. Your files never leave your Mac.

What Smoosh does

Smoosh compresses images and videos on your Mac using the open source libraries libwebp and libvpx, which are bundled into the app. All processing happens on your device, on the files you explicitly drop onto the app window.

What Smoosh does not do

  • Collect any personal information
  • Send files, metadata, or usage data to any server
  • Use analytics, telemetry, crash reporters, or tracking
  • Use advertising identifiers
  • Require an account, sign-in, or network connection
  • Read files you don't explicitly drop onto the app

File access

Smoosh runs inside Apple's App Sandbox with user-selected file access only. It can read and write the specific files you drop onto the window and the output folder you choose. It cannot reach any other files on your Mac.

Third-party services

None. Smoosh has no backend, no third-party SDKs, and no network requests.

Children's privacy

Smoosh does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+.

Contact

Questions? Email support@mysticcoders.com.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. If Smoosh ever begins collecting any data (it doesn't today), users will be notified in advance and the change will be disclosed in the App Store listing.
