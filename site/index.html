What it does
Image to WebP

Drop PNG, JPEG, TIFF, BMP, GIF, or HEIC files. Get optimized WebP output with visually lossless compression.

Default: 80% quality
Video to MP4

Downspec oversized video to sane dimensions with H.264 encoding. Ideal for web, ads, and IFE content.

Default: 1920px / 30fps / 2.0 Mbps
Video to WebM

VP9 encoding with a built-in WebM muxer. Open format, excellent compression, no ffmpeg required.

Default: 1920px / 30fps / 2.0 Mbps
How it works
Drop your files

Drag images or videos onto the app window or the Dock icon. Handles single files or batches.

Sensible defaults apply

Files convert immediately with production-tested settings. Adjust quality, resolution, or bitrate if needed.

Compressed files appear

Output saves next to the original (or a folder you choose). See file size reduction at a glance.
